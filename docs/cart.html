Cart

app/libraries/cart.php

This library is used to store items in a cart, update them, and remove them.

Methods

add_product($id, $qty)
Static. Add the product into the cart. If the product already exists, increase the quantity by $qty
remove_product($id)
Static. Remove an item from the cart.
set_quantity($id, $qty)
Static. Modify the quantity of an item in the cart.
get_total()
Static. Get the total quantity of items in the cart.
get_cart()
Static. Return the cart as an associative array.
clear_cart()
Static. Removes all items from the cart.
